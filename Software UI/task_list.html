Broadcast Tasks

TASKS WILL BE PICKED AND SENT OUT MOMENTARILY!

Tasks are generated here from the layout created on the last page. Our system can automatically detect how to create tasks based on the items that are placed and the amount of people available on set. Custom tasks can also be created using the "Custom Task" command. When the "Broadcast Tasks" command is chose, all the tasks are delegated to crew members who are given an auditory text to speech describing what they must do. They can repeat these commands by pushing a button on their headsets. Tasks are made up of requirements. You can see an example of how a task is laid out in our system by clicking "Install Camera"
