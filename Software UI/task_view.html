This page shows an example of the "Install Camera" task. Based on critera we gave this camera while creating the scene (proximity to other objects, the location, and the angle), specific requirements are automatically generated. A user of this system can very easily see who is responsible for doing a task and can send messages to their headset by holding down "Send a Message." The requirements of a task will be read to the person doing it one at as a time and tehy can push butttons on their headset to mark them as complete and move on to the next requirement. Specific custom requirements can also be generated using the "Add a custom requirement" button.
